Welcome to TrendsPlus ("we," "our," "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our mobile application, TrendsPlus (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information Collection and Use

We are committed to user privacy and minimize data collection.

a) Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

We do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information from you. This includes your name, email address, phone number, or physical address. The App does not require you to create an account or log in.

b) Non-Personally Identifiable Information (Non-PII)

To improve the performance, stability, and user experience of our App, we may collect anonymous, non-personally identifiable information, such as:

  • Device Information: Your device model, operating system version, and screen resolution.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, such as which content categories you view most frequently.
  • Crash Reports & Diagnostics: Anonymous crash reports and performance data to help us identify and fix bugs.

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

The App's core functionality is to display publicly available trending content from third-party services. We do not control the data provided by these sources. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies:
