Privacy Policy for TrendsPlus

Effective Date: August 21, 2025

TrendsPlus is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles user data.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or phone number.

The app only fetches publicly available trending content from third-party sources such as:

  • YouTube API (for trending videos)
  • Reddit API (for Reddit posts)
  • News API (for news and blogs)

Advertisements

Currently, the app does not display ads. In future updates, we may integrate Google AdMob or other advertising platforms. At that time, advertisements may be shown, and ad networks may use cookies or device identifiers to serve relevant ads.

Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links or content provided by third-party services (YouTube, Reddit, NewsAPI). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them:

  • YouTube / Google Privacy Policy
  • Reddit Privacy Policy
  • NewsAPI Privacy Policy

Future Updates

If login or account features are added in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy to explain how user data will be collected, used, and protected.
